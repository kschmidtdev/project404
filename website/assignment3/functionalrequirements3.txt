<p><strong>Name:</strong> Main Options </p><p><strong>Description:</strong> The user accesses the Options Menu (before game) </p><p><strong>Actors:</strong> ALL </p><p><strong>Use Case:</strong> </p><h3></h3><p><i>Normal:</i> </p><p>1. Selection: Move the cursor to Options and enter </p><p>2. Selection: Select volume bar, move to left for less or right for more. </p><p>3. Validate: Select Apply Changes. </p><p><i>Abnormal 1:</i> </p><p>1. Selection: Move the cursor to Options and enter </p><p>2. Selection: Select volume bar, move to left for less or right for more. </p><p>3. Reset: User makes an error, selects “use defaults?</p><p><strong>Features Used:</strong> </p><ul><li>Selection Cursor </li><li>Menu </li><li>Button </li></ul><p><strong>Exceptions:</strong> </p><ul><li>Incorrect volume, allow user to reset to defaults </li></ul><p><strong>Actors:</strong> </p><p><i>Max:</i> Max doesn&#x27;t care about the volume in game, heck he has speakers. He supposes if the AI became an option he&#x27;d find this more useful, but at the moment it seems frivolous at best </p><p><i>Hubert:</i> Hubert doesn&#x27;t like to multi task. Generally if he is focusing on something new he gives it his full attention. Thus including the volume and other options at this level suits him just fine. </p>
